## V1 Surfaces

### Fallback texture selection

`fallback_shadow_texture_selector_v1` narrows fallback texture words to state-coherent families derived from spectral entropy, pressure risk, distinguishability loss, typed `texture_signature`, and Shadow-v3 or `shadow_field` labels. Words such as `restless`, `muffled`, `settled`, `viscous`, `bright`, and `lattice` are no longer treated as interchangeable proof of texture.

The fallback contract still keeps the entropy sentence cap and the final standalone `NEXT: LISTEN` rule. The new point is quality: compact fallback should preserve the state's grain, not satisfy the prompt with a random texture token.

`dynamic_entropy_pressure_density_gradient_v1` extends the selector from a static family pick into deterministic texture ranking. The selector now parses `spectral_entropy`, `pressure_risk`, `density_gradient`, `mode_packing`, `semantic_friction`, and `distinguishability_loss`, then ranks weighted terms such as `viscous`, `muffled`, `lattice`, `restless`, `heavy`, `settled`, `shimmering`, and `bright`. The prompt receives the top terms and compact weights as guidance, not as mandatory wording. The old `texture_family` and `preferred_texture_terms` fields remain present for compatibility.

This dynamic weighting is language-context only. It does not change MLX behavior, force fallback, grant prompt priority, change telemetry priority, write codec dimensions, or touch controller/pressure/fill/PI policy.

### High-resonance descriptor preservation

Astrid's `introspection_astrid_llm_1782751667` also named a separate fallback risk: compactness can strip the "humming" quality of high resonance even when texture families are weighted correctly. `fallback_continuity_budget_v1` now parses `resonance_density` and, when it is high (`>=0.80`), records `resonance_descriptor_policy=preserve_resonance_or_humming_inside_existing_cap`.

This policy permits one resonance or humming descriptor inside the existing sentence cap. It does not increase `max_prose_sentences`, force fallback, or grant any runtime authority.

### Schema and heartbeat truth

`spectral_fingerprint_v1` remains the canonical typed packet. `spectral_fingerprint_integrity_v1` records whether typed data was present, whether legacy 32D compatibility was used, whether typed fields took precedence over legacy slots, and whether malformed vectors were detected.

Pressure and heartbeat boundary tests lock the exact behavior around pressure deltas, fill deltas, 4999/5000 ms timing, stale packets, reconnects, and active connection IDs. This protects the distinction between real stillness and stale hearing.

### Witness relational friction

`witness_relational_friction_v1` is interpretive context for Witness/status surfaces. It can classify available chamber context as:
