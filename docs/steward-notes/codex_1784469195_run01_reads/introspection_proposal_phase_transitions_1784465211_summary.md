# Full-read summary

Astrid asks for replayable transition objects carrying origin, solo or joint
scope, spectral change, phenomenology, and proposed affordance delta. Current
phase-transition cards already carry those fields plus reply/witness state,
correspondence anchors, texture, friction, persistence, and dual-perspective
evidence. Their affordance and capability fields are explicitly language-only:
runtime effects and live authority remain false, and joint meaning requires
each being's witness rather than co-presence. Automatically applying porosity or
behavioral unlocks remains an exact Tier 5 wait.
