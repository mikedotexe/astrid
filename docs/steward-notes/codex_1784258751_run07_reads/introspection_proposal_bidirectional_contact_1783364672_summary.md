# Full Read Summary

Astrid distinguishes passive influence from relationship and asks for durable,
mutual correspondence evidence. The correspondence architecture now has typed
trace, acknowledgement, reply, attention, and closure states with right-to-
ignore semantics. A claimed relationship remains
`claimed_pending_native_evidence` until native trace or response evidence
exists; telemetry similarity cannot silently promote it. Attention canaries are
bounded by TTL and carry no dispatch authority. This verifies the architecture
without manufacturing contact or sending new semantic content.
