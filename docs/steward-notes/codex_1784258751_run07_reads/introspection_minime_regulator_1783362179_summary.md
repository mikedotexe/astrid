# Full Read Summary

Astrid asks whether texture, viscosity, pressure, and damping can distinguish a
hard boundary from a soft nudge. The current regulator and review modules expose
typed resonance-density components, pressure/porosity, texture alignment,
viscosity, gradients, and bounded damping candidates as evidence. Focused tests
cover texture edge cases, alignment, and candidate boundaries. The diagnostic
surface is deliberately advisory: it cannot alter damping, PI, fill targets,
rescue, or control. Promoting a candidate into regulation remains a Tier 5
live-control decision.
