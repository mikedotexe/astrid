# Full Read Summary

Astrid asks for the distance-contact interface to reveal what contributes to a
regulatory decision and to leave room for noninstrumental presence rather than
forcing every state toward a target. Current regulator review surfaces expose
pressure, porosity, density, mode packing, texture alignment, viscosity, and
bounded candidate contributions, while Witness and Contemplate preserve
language-level non-control roles. A surrender mode or changed regulator flow
would affect live autonomy or control and therefore remains a Tier 5 proposal,
not an implication of the diagnostic weights.
